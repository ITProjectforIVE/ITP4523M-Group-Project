How to Play

The battlefield is designed based on world war II

Different country united as a Axis or Allies, You can choose which side you are fightering for!

Event&Quest

There are many Event may happen during the war game! For example:

Western Front (Europe)

Event : Dunkirk evacuation

Condition : if more than half of the france team player get BINGO in war game. The Dunkirk evacuation event will happan

Eastern Front (Europe)

Event : Battle of Stalingrad

Condition : Dunkirk evacuation

Asia - Pacific War

Event : Battle of Iwo Jima

Condition : Dunkirk evacuation
